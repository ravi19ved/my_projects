### Recent Research and Observations on Upwelling Along the Indian Coast

The topic of upwelling along the Indian coast, particularly observed using glider-based technology, hasn't seen recent dedicated research publications. However, there are numerous studies and reports oriented around the phenomenon of upwelling using other methodologies off the southwest coast of India.

#### Indexed Resources from Research Portals:

1. **Spatio-temporal Variability of Upwelling**
   - *Abstract*: Explores how satellite data on winds and sea levels help assess upwelling impacts along the Indian southwest coast.
   - *Source*: [ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0278434317301346).
  
2. **Coastal Upwelling Dynamics**
   - *Abstract*: Analyzes simulated temperature data to illustrate upwelling signals and temperature anomalies.
   - *Source*: [Taylor & Francis](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/17538947.2016.1216614).

3. **Climatological Approach to Upwelling**
   - *Abstract*: Uses a climatological framework to examine temperature anomalies indicating upwelling in the summer monsoon period.
   - *Source*: [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/281467194_Study_of_Upwelling_along_the_West_Coast_of_India-A_Climatological_Approach).

4. **Modeling and Observations of Upwelling (2001-2010)**
   - *Abstract*: Deals with intermonthly variability determined by model simulations over a ten-year period.
   - *Source*: [Bora B](https://bora.uib.no/bora-xmlui/handle/1956/9731).

5. **Impact of Indian Ocean Dipole on Upwelling**
   - *Abstract*: Demonstrates the effects of the Indian Ocean Dipole events on the upwelling features through model experiments.
   - *Source*: [Springer](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10236-018-1152-x).

### Conclusion

The phenomenon of upwelling along India's coast is crucial for understanding regional marine dynamics, particularly under the influence of the Indian monsoon system. Modern glider-based technology, while gaining traction in other parts of the world, represents a groundbreaking opportunity for future detailed analysis of such coastal processes in India. Moreover, integrating satellite data, modeling, and climatological studies, as explored in existing research, can significantly enhance insights into coastal upwelling mechanisms.
